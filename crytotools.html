About these ciphers

Caesar — Simple letter-shift by a fixed key. Educational, easily breakable.

Vigenère — Polyalphabetic shift using a keyword. Stronger than Caesar but vulnerable to frequency analysis.

AES-256 — Industry-standard symmetric encryption. Used globally for securing data at rest and in transit.
